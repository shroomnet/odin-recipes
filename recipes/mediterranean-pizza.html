Mediterranean Pizza

Description

Dive right into the deliciousness of a homemade Mediterranean Pizza! Picture this: a rich oil-based sauce, a cheesy blanket of mozzarella, and a vibrant medley of artichoke hearts, sundried tomatoes and zesty kalamata olives. Oh, and the pièce de résistance? Crumbly feta cheese on top. It's like a taste of the Mediterranean, right at your fingertips.

Ingredients

  • 10 oz ball pizza dough
  • ⅓ cup white all purpose flour
  • 1 jar sundried tomatoes in oil
  • 1 jar sliced kalamata olives
  • 1 can artichoke hearts
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 oz feta cheese block
  • 1 tbsp. crushed garlic
  • ½ tsp. red chili flakes
  • 2-3 dried bay leaves

Steps

  1. If not already, allow dough to rest, reaching room temperature before stretching.
  2. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (218 degrees C).
  3. Strain oil from the sundried tomatoes into a seperate container, setting tomoatoes aside. Combine garlic, chili flakes and bay leaves with the oil. Let marinate for 20-30 minutes, then remove bayleaves.
  4. Place dough on a clean, flat, nonporous surface. By hand, work dough into the general shape of your pan. Once satisfied, apply a generous amount of flour to each side of the dough ball. Using a rolling pin, roll out the dough until it is large enough to fit the entire surface of the pan.
  5. Apply a conservative amount of oil to the pizza dough, using a silicone brush to evenly distribute the oil. Make sure to cover the entire surface of the dough, crust included.
  6. Evenly spread mozzarella cheese, covering the entire center of the dough, right up to the edge of the crust.
  7. Working from the edge of the crust, into the center of the pizza, begin evenly distributing artichoke hearts, sundried tomatoes and kalamata olives.
  8. Crumble a generous amount of feta, and sprinkle over top of pizza.
  9. Place pizza on the bottom rack of the preheated oven and set a timer for 25 minutes, turning pan halfway through.
  10. Remove pizza from the oven, place on cutting board and allow to cool for 2-5 minutes before cutting. Enjoy!
