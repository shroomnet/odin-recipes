Mediterranean Pizza

Description

Dive right into the deliciousness of a homemade Mediterranean Pizza! Picture this: a rich oil-based sauce, a cheesy blanket of mozzarella, and a vibrant medley of artichoke hearts, sundried tomatoes and zesty kalamata olives. Oh, and the pièce de résistance? Crumbly feta cheese on top. It's like a taste of the Mediterranean, right at your fingertips.

Ingredients

  • 10 oz ball pizza dough
  • 1 jar sundried tomatoes in oil
  • 1 jar sliced kalamata olives
  • 1 can artichoke hearts
  • 1 oz feta cheese block
  • 1 tbsp. crushed garlic
  • ½ tsp. red chili flakes
  • 2-3 dried bay leaves
